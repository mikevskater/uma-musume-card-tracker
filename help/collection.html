Collection Management

Track your owned cards, set their levels and limit breaks, and manage your collection data.

Adding Cards to Collection

Mark as Owned: Check the checkbox in the "Owned" column for any card you own.
📷 Ownership checkbox screenshot
Automatic Defaults: New owned cards default to Level 30 (LB 2).
Visual Changes: Owned cards get a green highlight and updated controls.

Setting Levels and Limit Breaks

For each owned card, you can set:

  • Current Level: The card's current level (affects effect calculations)
  • Limit Break: Current limit break level (LB 0-4)
📷 Level and LB controls screenshot
💡 Level Validation: The app automatically prevents setting levels higher than the current limit break allows.

Detailed Card Management

Click any card to open the detailed view where you can:

  • Toggle ownership status
  • Set precise levels with validation
  • Adjust limit break levels
  • View calculated effect values at current level
📷 Card detail modal screenshot

Global Level Controls

Use the "Level Display Options" in the sidebar for analysis:

🔧 Set All LB

Override all cards to use the same limit break level for comparison purposes.

👤 Apply to Owned

Choose whether the global LB setting affects your owned cards or uses their individual levels.

📈 Max Potential

Show effect values at maximum level for each card's current limit break.

Data Management

Protect and share your collection data:

Export Collection: Download your owned cards data as a JSON file.
📷 Data management buttons screenshot
Import Collection: Load previously exported collection data.
Clear Collection: Remove all ownership data (with confirmation).
💾 Auto-Save: All changes to your collection are automatically saved to your browser's local storage.

Collection Analysis

Use filters and sorting to analyze your collection:

  • Filter by "Owned Only" to see just your cards
  • Sort by level to identify cards that need upgrading
  • Use effect filters to find your strongest cards
  • Compare multiple owned cards to optimize team building
⚠️ Browser Data: Collection data is stored in your browser. Clearing browser data or using incognito mode will not persist your collection.
