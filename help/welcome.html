Welcome to the Uma Musume Support Card Tracker

This tool helps you manage and analyze your support card collection with advanced filtering, sorting, and comparison features.

Key Features

  • Card Collection Management: Track owned cards with levels and limit breaks
  • Advanced Filtering: Filter by rarity, type, effects, skills, and more
  • Multi-Layer Sorting: Sort by multiple criteria with customizable priority
  • Card Comparison: Side-by-side comparison of multiple cards
  • Data Import/Export: Backup and share your collection data
  • Dark Mode: Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition
💡 Getting Started: Click on any card row to view detailed information, or use the comparison mode to select multiple cards for analysis.
