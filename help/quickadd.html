Quick Add Cards

Rapidly register and update your card collection one card at a time in a focused full-screen interface.

Getting Started

Step 1: Click the "Quick Add Cards" button in the sidebar, located above the Filters section.
Step 2: Configure your filters on the setup screen to narrow down which cards to review.
Step 3: Click "START" to begin the card-by-card flow.

Setup Screen

The setup screen lets you configure exactly which cards to review:

  • Region Toggle: Global (default) shows only globally released cards. JP shows all cards including JP-only releases.
  • Card Type Filters: Tap type icons to toggle ‚Äî Speed, Stamina, Power, Guts, Wit, Friend. Use Select All / Deselect All for quick bulk changes.
  • Rarity Filters: Tap rarity icons ‚Äî R, SR, SSR. Also supports Select All / Deselect All.
  • Ownership Filter: Choose from All Cards, Owned Only, or Unowned Only.
  • Sort Order: Alphabetical (with rarity tiebreak), By Type, By Rarity, or By Release Date.
  • Card Count: Live preview showing how many cards match your current filters.
üí° Tip: Use the Unowned Only filter with Alphabetical sort to quickly register new cards you just pulled.

Progress & Navigation

  • Progress Bar: Shows your current position (e.g., "Card 5 of 120").
  • Prev / Next Arrows: Navigate between cards. Wraps around from last to first and vice versa.
  • Skip Owned Toggle: When enabled, prev/next navigation skips already-owned cards ‚Äî useful for registering only new cards.
