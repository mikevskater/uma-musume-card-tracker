Glossary & Quick Reference

Quick reference for game terms, abbreviations, and mechanics used throughout the app.

Training Types

🏃 Speed

Affects position-taking and final stretch acceleration. Primary stat for front runners and stalkers.

💪 Stamina

Determines how far a character can run at full effort. Critical for long-distance runners.

⚡ Power

Affects acceleration and ability to push through packs. Important for chasers and betweeners.

🔥 Guts

Affects stamina conservation and late-race willpower. Helps in close finishes and last spurt.

🧠 Wisdom

Affects race strategy execution and skill activation rate. Also recovers energy during training.

Stats & Abbreviations

SP — Skill Points

Currency used to learn skills during training. Earned from Wisdom training and card effects.

LB — Limit Break

Card upgrade level (0–4). Higher LB increases max level and effect values. LB 4 = max level 50 for SSR.

Train Eff — Training Effectiveness

Percentage bonus applied to all training stat gains. Stacks from multiple cards.

Race Bonus

Percentage bonus to fan gains and rewards from races. A key competitive metric.

Friend Bonus

Multiplier applied when training with a card at max friendship. Higher values mean bigger stat boosts from friendship training.

Energy Recovery

Reduces the energy (stamina) cost of training. Helps sustain more training sessions without rest.

Card Properties

⭐ Rarity

SSR (max Lv 50) — strongest effects and unique abilities.
SR (max Lv 45) — solid mid-tier cards.
R (max Lv 40) — basic cards with lower effect values.

🏷️ Card Type

Determines which training the card appears at: Speed, Stamina, Power, Guts, Wisdom, or Friend (flexible placement).

📊 Effects

Passive bonuses provided by the card (e.g., Speed Bonus +15%, Training Eff +10%). Values scale with card level.

🎯 Hint Skills

Skills the card teaches at reduced SP cost during training. The hint discount depends on the card's Hint Effect level.

✨ Unique Effects

Special abilities exclusive to SSR cards that activate at specific limit break levels. Only one unique effect per card.

Game Mechanics

😊 Mood Levels

Very Good: +20% stat gains
Good: +10%
Normal: ±0%
Bad: −10%
Very Bad: −20%

🏟️ Facility Levels

Training facility upgrades (Lv 1–5) that increase base stat gains and modify failure rates. Higher = better training results.

🤝 Friendship Training

Activated when a support card reaches max friendship. Provides a significant stat multiplier during training, boosted by the card's Friend Bonus effect.

📈 Aptitude Grades

Character-specific letter grades (S/A/B/C/D/E/F/G) for surfaces (Turf/Dirt) and distances (Short/Mile/Medium/Long). Higher grades improve race performance in matching conditions.

💥 Failure Rate

Chance of training failure (injury). Increases with lower energy. Shown at base energy ≥ 50 in the training breakdown. Wisdom training has 0% failure rate.

📋 Growth Rates

Character-specific percentage multipliers that scale how much each stat grows per training. A 120% growth rate means 20% more gains than baseline.
